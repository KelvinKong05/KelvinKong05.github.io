Who are we?

Get ready to witness the remarkable dedication and passion of our club's talented membership as they embark on a thrilling journey to preserve and breathe life into our rich industrial, agricultural, and maritime mechanical heritage. Brace yourself for a captivating experience as these extraordinary volunteers restore magnificent engines and meticulously operate a museum that encapsulates the essence of our vibrant past. Join us as we delve into a world where history comes alive through the tireless efforts of these remarkable individuals.

Experience Like No Other

The museum offers an unparalleled view of a diverse collection of vintage machinery. Its exhibits have gained recognition from Engineers Australia, who have honored the museum with an Engineering Heritage Marker. These engines, meticulously collected and restored by club members over the years, are accompanied by privately owned engines from club members, adding to the center's comprehensive display.

The transformed grounds offer an enchanting park atmosphere, adorned with inviting picnic tables nestled under the cool shade of majestic trees.
